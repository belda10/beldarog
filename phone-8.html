ROG PHONE 8

The ASUS ROG Phone 8 is a high-performance gaming smartphone featuring a 6.78-inch AMOLED display with a 165Hz refresh rate, powered by the Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 processor, and up to 16GB of RAM for smooth and responsive gameplay. It comes with a 6,000mAh battery, 65W fast charging, and advanced cooling to ensure optimal performance during extended gaming sessions. With customizable AirTriggers, immersive stereo speakers, and a premium design, the ROG Phone 8 delivers an outstanding gaming experience.

ROG PHONE 8 PRO

The ASUS ROG Phone 8 Pro is a flagship gaming smartphone that combines a 6.78-inch AMOLED display with a 165Hz refresh rate, powered by the Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 chipset, and up to 18GB of RAM for smooth, lag-free gaming. It features advanced cooling with the GameCool 6 system, a 6,000mAh battery with 65W fast charging, and a customizable rear OLED display for personalized aesthetics. Equipped with AirTriggers, dual front-firing speakers, and a versatile camera system, the ROG Phone 8 Pro is designed to deliver exceptional performance and immersive gaming experiences.
